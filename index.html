About

I am currently pursuing a PhD in Computer Science at the University of Hildesheim, where I work in the Data Science Group. My research focuses on spiking neural networks for transformer architectures, time series forecasting and classification, and practical machine learning for industry applications.

I hold an M.Sc. in Data Analytics (University of Hildesheim), an M.Sc. in Financial Mathematics (The University of Texas at Dallas), and a B.Sc. in Industrial Engineering (Bilkent University).

Open to research areas:

  • Time Series Forecasting
  • Computer Vision
